ENVIRONMENTAL ISSUES

CLIMATE CHANGE

Climate change is the change in the normal natural climatic conditions due to anthropogenic activities. This environmental issue has had a destructive impact on the earth such as melting of glaciers, epidemics, riding sea level.

Read more

GLOBAL WARMING

Global warming is an increase in the average temperature of the earth’s atmosphere. It is caused by burning of fossil fuels, emissions from automobiles and CFCs (Chlorofluorocarbons). Today, the world is in peril because of global warming.

Read more

DEFORESTATION

Deforestation is the depletion of trees and other forest resources. Due to this serious environmental problem, there has been a drastic reduction in the amount of oxygen present in the atmosphere and destruction of habitats.

Read more

OZONE LAYER DEPLETION

Ozone layer is a part of the atmosphere – precisely the stratosphere – that houses concentrated Ozone gas (O3). This gas protects the earth from the violet ultraviolet rays of the sun but it is now being destroyed due to the emission of CFCs.

Read more

DESERTIFICATION

Otherwise known as desert encroachment, desertification is the process by which fertile lands become dry and subsequently deserts due to deforestation, drought or inappropriate farming.

Read more

WATER POLLUTION

As the name implies, water pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into water bodies (Oceans, seas, lakes and many more) which changes the physical, chemical or biological condition of the water bosies. This could lead to severe consequences.

Read more

SOLUTIONS TO THE ISSUES

SUSTAINABILITY

Sustainability is the number one solution to environmental issues. To achieve this, natural resources must be used at a rate in which they can be replenished; such as planting five trees in return for cutting down one. 

Learn More

GO FOR REUSABLE

Pollution in forms such as that of air, water and land can be reduced by using reusable products instead of disposables. For example, using ceramic and stainless plates instead of disposable papers and nylons.  

Learn More

RECYCLING

Recycling is the act of processing abandoned or used products for use in creating entirely new products. This is an industry in which governments and private bodies should invest in as it helps in reducing pollution. 

Learn More

RENEWABLE ENERGY

Instead of using nonrenewable energy products such as gas, renewable energy such as solar energy should be considered in order to reduce emission of gasses such as CO and CO2 into the atmosphere.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

 Do human activities contribute to CC? 

Human activities such as burning of fossil fuels and those that cause emission of Greenhouse gasses (GHGs) and aerosols contribute to change in the natural climate. 

 What is ozone layer? 

The ozone layer is a layer of gas found in the atmosphere, precisly in the troposhere, that protects the earth against ultraviolet rays of the sun. It is made up of Ozone gas (O3).

 Can the earth be saved from CC? 

Yes. The earth can be saved from climate change if all hands are put on desk and all minds focused on eliminating climate change by following laid down principles and creating awareness. It begins with me and you.  

 What are the impacts of climate change?

The impacts of climate change include but not limited to long lasting heat waves, easier spread of disease, loss of habitats and rise in sea levels.

What is ecocide?

Ecocide is the deliberate or uncaring destruction of the natural environment.
